How Color Theory Works

Color theory uses relationships between hues on the color wheel to create aesthetically pleasing combinations. We analyze your skin tone and recommend clothing based on these harmony types:

Complementary

Colors opposite on the wheel (e.g., blue & orange) provide bold contrast and visual energy.

Analogous

Colors next to each other (e.g., green, teal, blue) feel smooth and natural together.

Triadic

Three evenly spaced hues (e.g., red, yellow, blue) offer vibrant, balanced contrast.

Monochromatic

Different shades and tones of the same hue create elegant, cohesive outfits.
